Welcome to Trade Binder!

Trade Binder is a webapp designed to help you keep track of your Pokemon Card collection. Current features include: searching cards, ability to view your collection or wishlist, and directly add a card to your collection via form. You may also add cards directly to your collection/wishlist from the search results and you may remove a card from your collection using the button provided on the collection page.
